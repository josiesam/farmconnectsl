FarmConnect - Empowering Farmers through Digital Market Access

Project Objective

FarmConnect is a technology-driven initiative designed to enhance the well-being of small-scale farmers in Sierra Leone. The primary goal of this project is to empower farmers by providing real-time market price information and establishing a digital marketplace connecting them directly with buyers. The project aims to boost transparency, reduce intermediary costs, and enable farmers to make informed decisions about their agricultural produce

Challenges addressed

Small-scale farmers in Sierra Leone encounter multiple challenges, including limited access to market data and dependency on intermediaries. FarmConnect seeks to address these challenges by unifying two essential components in one digital platform

Project Components

  • Market Price Information

    FarmConnect's Market Price Information System will deliver real-time market prices for various agricultural commodities. This valuable data will be accessible to farmers via an intuitive mobile application or web platform. The core objective of this component is to arm farmers with actionable information, enabling them to make well-informed decisions regarding when, where, and at what price to sell their crops

  • Electronic Marketplace

    The second facet of FarmConnect will introduce an electronic marketplace where farmers can directly list their produce for sale, connecting them seamlessly with potential buyers. This digital platform will facilitate secure communication and negotiation between farmers and buyers, streamlining direct transactions. By eliminating intermediaries and reducing the time it takes to bring produce to market, this component strives to elevate farmers' income and profitability.
